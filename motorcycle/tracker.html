TRACKER

At first glance the Tracker style looks very similar to the Café-Racer but actually the Tracker style is a design that comes from American dirt track racing lads. So the Tracker style is identical to the dual purpose designed to traverse the ground and asphalt as opposed to the Moto GP motorbike which is imitated by the Café Racer style.

TRACKER

Usually Trackers have a single seat and a more upright sitting position for the rider. The most famous Tracker is the one driven by Evel Knievel
