BOBBER

In general, excess weight on the motorbike is usually removed or cut to reduce the load on the motorbike. All unnecessary hardware is usually removed to reduce weight including the exhaust in most cases and is usually a single seater.

BOBBER

Old school handlebars are usually used on custom bobber motorbikes. Cables are also often installed on the inside so that the appearance looks cleaner. Harley is the brand most often associated with the bobber style, but we also see many motorbikes such as Trump and India. But nowadays there are also many motorbikes such as Byson or naked motorbikes that are made into custom bobber motorbikes.
